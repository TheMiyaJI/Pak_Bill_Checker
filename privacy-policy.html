Pak Bill Checker Privacy Policies About

Pak Bill Checker is a smart utility app that allows users to quickly and easily check electricity bills for all Pakistani power companies online. With features like WAPDA Bill, Power Bill Checker, Online Bill Checking, and Pakistani Utility Bills, it provides a fast and reliable service for hassle-free bill inquiries.

Note: This app is not affiliated with any government organization; it only provides direct links to official billing portals for user convenience.

Disclaimer: The app doesn’t represent a government entity.

Our app is merely a source of showing electricity bills to the users for their convenience. We only serve the purpose of an online electricity bill viewer. Our app is solely made for the convenience of users. The data shown in the app is available online for the general public.

The sources of the information are:

  • PITC Bill
  • KE Staging

We do not own or claim copyrights of the data shown in our app.

User Privacy

Our app does not collect any user data, such as usernames, email addresses, or passwords. It is completely secure and ensures user privacy.

User Permissions

This app requires internet access to retrieve electricity bill details securely. No personal data is collected, stored, or shared.

Children Privacy Policies

This app does not violate any child privacy policies and does not collect data from children.

We may change our Policies in the future, please keep yourself updated.

ڈسکلیمر: ایپ کسی سرکاری ادارے کی نمائندگی نہیں کرتی۔

معلومات کے ذرائع یہ ہیں:

  • آئی ٹی سی بل
